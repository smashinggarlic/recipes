# Pappu Chaaru amma style

Brine the chicken in diluted salt water (room temp) for 45 mins before the start.


## Pappu prep
Pressure cook the daal for 3 whistles


# Masala Ingredients

- Cumin - 1 tsp
- Mustard seeds - pinch
- Cinnamon - 3/4 stick
- Cloves - 2 
- Cardamom - 2 
- Rock flower - 2 pinches (optional)
- Methi seeds - pinch
- Bay leaf
- One red chilli
