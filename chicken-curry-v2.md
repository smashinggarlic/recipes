# Chicken Curry

Brine the chicken in diluted salt water (room temp) for 45 mins before the start.

##  Ingredients
 - Chicken (thighs preferrable) - 1lb ish
 - mint leaves
 - 1 green chilli 
 - Chilli powder 
 - Dhaniya powder
 - Turmeric
 - salt
 - yogurt (not too much)
 - Ginger garlic paste

## Whole Spices
- Clove - 1
- Cardamom - 1
- Cinnamon - small stick
- cumin seeds - pinch

## Masala Ingredients
 - Peppercorns  - 0.5 tsp
 - coriander seeds - tbsp
 - Cumin - 1 tsp
 - Cinnamon - 3/4 stick
-  Cloves - 2 
-  Fennel - 0.5 tsp
-  Cardamom - 2 
 - Rock flower - 2 pinches (optional)

## Masala prep

Saute all the masala ingredients on low flame for few mins until flavor comes out. Let it cool down and ground them and keep it aside


## Steps

 1. In a heavy bottom pan, add oil, once hot add the whole spices
 2. When the cumin seeds splutter, add chopped mint and one/two green chillis
 3. Saute for 2 mins and add chopped onions (add pinch of salt)
 4. Saute onions until slight golden brown (nutty)
 5. Add ginger garlic paste (a spoon full)
 5. Add curry leaves, saute for few mins
 6. Add chilli powder, dhaniya powder, tumeric and salt. Cook for 3-4 mins under low-medium heat ()
 6. Add chicken, mix and let it cook until it slowly releases water 
 9. Add 2 tbsp of coconut powder/sesame powder for gravy thickness (optional)
 10. Add the beaten yogurt (half a cup), mix and cook it slow until oil comes up on top 
 11. Add the masala powder (add based on how much you want it) and mix well.
 12. Cook slow and nice with a lid on. Make adjustments based on your taste.
 13. Add chopped cilantro and serve
